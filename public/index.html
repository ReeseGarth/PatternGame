Simple Pattern!

Welcome to Simple Pattern!

The game is a simple memory game. When you are ready to start, click the start button and a random sequence of squares will fade out and then back in. Remember this sequence because once it has stopped playing it is your job to enter the exact same sequence. Each time you get the sequence correct you go to the next round, but if you guess wrong it's game over! The rounds also get progressively harder with faster and longer sequences. Have fun and good luck!
